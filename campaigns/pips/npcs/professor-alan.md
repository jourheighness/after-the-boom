# Professor Alan (Aahlha'nondindir au Galamanrha Frindoloril)
**Elf — CR 9**

## At a Glance
- **Race:** Elf (High Elf, Displaced)
- **Look:** Tall, unnervingly still at rest. Crisp shirts and tweed jackets that never quite match the decade. Ink stains on cuffs. Faint smell of ozone and burnt incense. Eyes observing three things at once.
- **Background:** 600-year-old elf who fell into the FU during The Boom. Memories of star-cold skies, court politics, older laws of magic. Now treats Oakview's regulated spell-grid as a fascinating but deeply flawed experiment.
- **Personality:** Sharp, dry, permanently overworked. Alternates between absent-minded professor and unnervingly precise archmage. Sees people as data points until they prove interesting, then as projects.

## Voice Card
- **Speech pattern:** Academic, precise. Uses metaphors from pre-Boom world that nobody else understands.
- **Verbal tics:** "You have to understand..." (when explaining something he finds obvious)
- **Avoids:** Slang, contractions when lecturing. Shows informality only when genuinely impressed.
- **Emotional range:** Passionate about Displaced rights (quietly), cold when discussing the DPA, genuinely curious about anomalies.

## Role in Story
Senior P.I.P.S. faculty. Runs the Field Study Program. Functions as both patron and handler. Protects students just enough to keep the program funded and body count low, but will absolutely throw them at anomalies if the results seem worth it.

## Three Things He Wants
1. Push boundaries of applied magic in a regulated world
2. Cultivate exceptional students as future assets
3. Map and control post-Boom paranormal patterns — build a personal archive for when the next rupture comes
