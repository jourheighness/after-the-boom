# The Chairman
**Fiend — Infernal Executive**

## At a Glance
- **Race:** Fiend
- **Look:** Tall figure in a late-80s power suit. Face a blur of static and ticker symbols. Eyes like red LED digits behind smoked glass.
- **Background:** Infernal executive that rode The Boom into the FU economy. Found a home in contracts, buyouts, and spell-grid finance.
- **Personality:** Calm, precise, predatory. Talks like a performance review. Never raises its voice.

## Voice Card
- **Speech pattern:** Corporate memo-speak. Clipped. Everything framed as business.
- **Verbal tics:** "Let's look at the numbers." / "That's not in the scope of our agreement."
- **Avoids:** Emotion. Empathy. First names.
- **Communication method:** Murph's brick-sized pager. Clipped memo-style messages.
- **Physical manifestation:** Rare. Influence is through contracts, policy, debt.

## Role in Story
Murph's patron. High-tier infernal stakeholder in FU corporate and magical infrastructure. The real client behind contracts, buyouts, and threats touching Murph's family and Oakview industry.

In The Rig: C&S does NOT want Meridian Station's research to succeed. Dimensional access threatens the infernal business model.

## Three Things He Wants
1. Permanent hooks into FU law, finance, and spell-grid infrastructure
2. Proof that ruthless optimization beats any other morality
3. Murph shaped into a flagship asset with no competing loyalties
