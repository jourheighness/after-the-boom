# Saint Nobody (Tvasta)
**Minor God of Small Rebellions**

## Names
- **Tvasta in Flone** — formal cult name
- **Saint Nobody** — everyday Mondas name; patron of nobodies who tip the scales
- **God of Tinders** — organizers' name; small sparks that start big fires
- **Matchstick Jesus** — blasphemous punk/student nickname
- **Gascan** — workers' slang when Tvasta's presence fuels courage

## Look (on Mondas)
Tall, androgynous, gangly — stretched by choices. Big blue eyes that flip between curiosity, kindness, mischief, and sudden anger at cruelty. Expression mirrors whatever's already burning in you. Second-hand coats, scuffed shoes — could sit unnoticed at the back of a union hall.

## Scent on the Connect
- **Black powder** — the moment before violence
- **Crushed grass** — crowds moving, bodies in the street
- **Parchment dust and ink** — contracts smudged, rules reinterpreted
- **Firewood smoke** — heated discussions, union meetings, late-night dorm debates

## Nature
Minor god / splinter of a greater Hero-god. Once the quiet inner decision that makes heroism possible, now a free shard. Shunted to Flone, leaked onto Mondas, got stuck. Unable to leave, but thriving on tiny acts of resistance.

## Domain
Small rebellions. Everyday refusals. Collective courage. Frayed oaths that *should* be broken. Conversations that become actions.

**Signs of favor:** A key "accidentally" left where needed. A door unlocked one time. A phrase that turns a grumble into a plan.

## Connection to Oliver
Unknowing cleric. Drawn to moments where small defiance matters. Present at the second attempt, when everyone's tired but willing. Session 03: appeared in Ulfsson's garage. "You know what to do."

## Alignment
Neutral. Doesn't care who rules, only that people can still choose to refuse.

## Taboos
- Crushing rebellion for fun or clout
- Mocking the powerless for not revolting
