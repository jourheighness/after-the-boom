# Campaign: Kids at P.I.P.S.

## Premise
Four young adults at P.I.P.S. in Oakview, North Ostreon. Coming-of-age in a world that exploits its own magic. Enrolled in Professor Alan's Field Study Program — do what he says, survive the semester, graduate.

## Themes
- Identity: What are we? Why are we special?
- Found family vs. blood family
- The personal IS political — you can't fight the DPA without saving Lena, can't save Lena without fighting the DPA
- Systems of extraction — the same exploitation, just with fire elementals instead of combustion engines
- Coming-of-age in a world that doesn't care about you

## Tone
90s rust-belt + urban fantasy + punk. Springsteen meets Stranger Things meets early X-Men. The DPA is ICE. Stabil-Tabs are OxyContin. The Boom is every systemic disruption that promised change and delivered bureaucracy.

## The Mother Structure
Four boys. Four mothers. One chance.

| PC | Mother | Status | Arc |
|---|---|---|---|
| Murph | Present but hollowed | Addict, destroyed by Stabil-Tabs — designed by his own father | No rescue. Only revenge or acceptance. |
| Brian | Gone | Deadbeat, chose to leave. Lesley raised him solo. | No rescue. Just absence. Chosen family vs blood. |
| Max | Taken | Alive. Held by DPA. Looked back through Grimsson's scrying. | THE mission. The one who can be saved. |
| Oliver | Present and whole | Eva raised him in the resistance. Parents fled Tierrasur coup. | The proof it doesn't have to go wrong. |

## Campaign State (Post-Session 3)
**Before:** "What are we? Why are we special? What does Alan want?"
**Now:** "They took Max's mom. They're watching Brian. They're using Murph. We have proof."

The DPA is confirmed rotten — not abstract bureaucracy but the thing that destroyed their families.

## Act Structure
**Act I** ends when they locate Lena and commit to going after her.
- Find where Lena is
- Understand what happened to her (is she still Lena?)
- Plan an extraction (federal facility, not a warehouse)
- Decide what happens after (can she come home?)
