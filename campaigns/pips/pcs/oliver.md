# Oliver Augustino
**Human — Cleric (of Saint Nobody)**

## Quick Profile
21 years old. Politically conscious. Human. Middle-class turned grinding working-class. Cleric — unknowing agent of Saint Nobody, minor god of small rebellions.

Grunge-punk. Grimey. Works at the family print shop (Stop n' Print). Socialist spirit — won't work corporate, wants to serve the public sphere. Tough political background, heard many horrors about how halflings are treated in the FU.

Little brother: Javier (gnome). Parents: Eva & Tobias Augustino — came before The Boom, fled Tierrasur (FU-backed coup). Since then, raised Oliver in the spirit of resistance.

**Academic Interest:** Magical ecology, magical fertilizer
**Song:** Bombtrack — Rage Against the Machine

## Character Arc
Oliver is the proof it doesn't have to go wrong. His mother is present and whole. Eva raised him in the resistance. He has a framework for what's happening — he's seen political persecution before, through his parents' stories of Tierrasur.

## Saint Nobody Connection
Unknowing cleric. Drawn to moments where small defiance matters more than big speeches. Often present at the second attempt at rebellion, when everyone's tired but still willing. Has a talent for standing in the exact place where the right person overhears the right sentence.

**Session 03:** Saint Nobody appeared in Ulfsson's garage after the DPA chase. Said only: "You know what to do." Not instruction — confirmation.

## Active Threads
- **Sarah De La Coeur:** Interested in him and his band. Let her down gently.
- **Leyline (the band):** Rehearses in Pointview Industrial. Connection to punk/resistance scene.
- **Javier:** Kid brother, hovers at the edge of PC business. Leverage point if enemies want pressure.
