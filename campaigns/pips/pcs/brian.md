# Brian "Yuno" McKnight
**Human — Paladin (Oath of Vengeance)**

## Quick Profile
19 years old. Grew up with his dad (Lesley), who worked as a maintenance worker at Black Creek Power Station. Mom (Maria) left when he was young — deadbeat, partier, alcohol problems. Dad is steady, quiet, stubborn.

After school, Brian worked at Black Creek. Magic was used to push energy production, safety levels constantly lowered. He didn't blow up over one accident — he blew up over the *system knowing someone would get hurt and continuing anyway*.

Open confrontation. Union got involved. A worker was injured during a meeting. The union saw his idealism and spine: "You have ryggrad — now you need the law." Sent to a union candidate program: safety, law, conflict resolution. Still floor-level, more practitioner than desk worker.

**Organization:** Union Electric & Spellworks / UBAEW ("You-Bae-You")
**Song:** The River — Bruce Springsteen

## Character Arc
His Vengeance comes from accountability — not punishment, but someone actually standing for consequences. Mother chose to leave. No rescue, just absence. Chosen family vs blood.
