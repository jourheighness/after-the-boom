# Max "Thorfinn" Ulfsson
**Human — Monk (Somatic Kinesiology / Physical Therapy)**

## Quick Profile
25 years old (oldest student). Insists on being called "Thorfinn" but it never sticks. Long ponytail, growing bald spot. Alt-rock soul-skater with a spiritual streak.

Faded denim jacket, tight jeans with knee holes, classic Converse scribbled with runes. Carries a skateboard everywhere. Yoga practitioner. Slightly crooked Vegvísir tattoo from a festival tent.

Grew up in The Sevens (Block 7), near the leyline substation. House constantly flickered — buzzing lights, vibrating cutlery, blue milk. Messy, loud, warm.

**Academic Track:** Dept. of Somatic Kinesiology & Exertion Wellness (Monk Track) — physical therapy, workplace ergonomics, meditation, unarmed conflict resolution.
**Why P.I.P.S.:** Helping injured skaters at the local park was the first time he felt genuinely useful. Wants to "heal with Viking energy."
**Song:** 18 and Life — Skid Row

## Parents
- **Father — Uffe Ulfsson:** Mechanic at Ulfsson Repairs. Fixes magically fried engines. Loves Viking history and cheap beer.
- **Mother — Lena Ulfsson:** Officially died of "cancer." Actually alive, held by the DPA. Cashier at Ley-Lite Liquors. Into Nordic spirituality, tarot, essential oils.

## Character Arc
THE mission. Lena is the one who can be saved. Max went from easygoing skater-yogi to someone with a reason to fight. Everything now orbits finding his mother.
