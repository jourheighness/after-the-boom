## ENCOUNTER SETUPS

### 1. BOILER BREAK -- The Sevens, Block 7
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Trigger:** Brian's sabotage destabilized the local grid. Power surges stressed the CTEs in nearby buildings. Two break containment.
**Location:** Mrs. Chen's building + alley behind Ulfsson Repairs. Night. Pipes screaming. Steam venting from basement windows. Smell of scorched copper.

**Enemies:** 2 CTE Ferals

**Setup:** Mrs. Chen's boiler spirit cracks its housing first — banging pipes, radiators glowing cherry-red, steam flooding the hallway. While the boys deal with that, a second CTE breaks out of the laundromat across the alley. Two scared, volatile elementals loose in a residential block. This isn't a fight — it's a containment job.

**Complications:**
- Neither CTE wants to fight. They're panicked. Calming one (RESONANCE DC 15 or Animal Handling DC 18) is the clean solution.
- The first CTE is in Mrs. Chen's basement — tight space, pipes everywhere, gas line nearby. Flare in here = building fire.
- The second CTE darts toward the damaged grid junction. If it reaches the sparking node, it absorbs the energy — heals to full, Flare recharges, becomes aggressive instead of scared.
- Mrs. Chen is on the stairs. She won't leave her building. Someone has to get her out or contain the CTE before Flare goes off.
- If the fight gets loud or fire starts, Agent Moss (canvassing nearby) arrives in 4 rounds. Now they're explaining why they're at the scene of more grid damage.

**Non-combat solutions:** Lure them with heat sources (car engine, space heater). Herd them back into a containment vessel (Ulfsson Repairs has welding tanks). Brian could jury-rig a temporary housing if he has tools (GRI DC 14, 2 rounds).

**Rewards:** +1 Heat if noisy/fire. Edge if Brian re-contains both. Mrs. Chen's trust if her building survives. Neighborhood goodwill — "those boys from the school fixed the boilers."

### 2. BADGE TROUBLE -- DPA Branch Office
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Trigger:** The PCs attempt to infiltrate the downtown DPA office for Lena's files.
**Location:** DPA Branch Office, downtown Oakview. After hours. Fluorescent lights, filing cabinets, government carpet.

**Enemies:** 2 DPA Field Agents (patrol) + 1 DPA Senior Agent (office, arrives round 3)

**Setup:** Two field agents on night patrol. One at the front desk, one walking the second floor. If the PCs trip an alarm (failed Stealth DC 14, or opening the restricted filing room DC 16 with tools), both agents converge. The Senior Agent arrives from the break room after 3 rounds -- she was doing paperwork late.

**Complications:**
- The filing room has a clicker alarm (DC 14 THAUMATECH to disable). Any thaumic use inside triggers it automatically.
- The senior agent has NEURAL LOCK. If she locks one PC, the other agents can cuff them.
- Escape routes: loading dock (ground floor), fire escape (2nd floor, rusty, MUS DC 12 to force), or front door (visible to street).
- If they get Lena's file: red tab, "SITE-11R," "SUSTAINED YIELD -- DO NOT TRANSFER."
- +2d6 Heat for getting caught. +1d6 even if clean but alarm tripped (logs).

**Non-combat option:** Social engineering during business hours. Murph talks his way in (Deception DC 15 vs receptionist, DC 18 vs senior agent). Oliver could forge a faculty research request (Creativity DC 14 + Forgery tools).

**Rewards:** Lena's routing code. Scale of the red-file program. Possible evidence for Free X.

### 3. MOTOR POOL MIDNIGHT -- DPA Fleet Depot
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Trigger:** Following Dag's lead to the DPA fleet depot for vehicle transport logs.
**Location:** Edge of town. Chain-link fence, guard booth, rows of vans and sedans. One fluorescent-lit garage. Oil stains, tire tracks.

**Enemies:** 1 DPA Field Agent (guard booth) + 2 Beat Cops (backup, arrive round 5 if alarm raised)

**Setup:** One agent in the booth, watching a small TV. Cameras cover the main gate and garage entrance. Two cameras are broken (Dag told them which). A gap in the fence on the north side (Perception DC 12 to find, or Dag tells them).

**Complications:**
- The transport logs are in a clipboard rack inside the garage office. Not locked, just messy. Finding the right log: Search DC 12 (10 minutes) or DC 16 (2 minutes).
- A guard dog (Mastiff stats, HP 10, AC 12) sleeps near the garage. Wakes on noise (Stealth DC 13). Can be bribed with food (Animal Handling DC 10).
- If the alarm goes, the agent locks the gate and calls Oakview PD. Two beat cops arrive in 5 rounds. Not DPA -- just local police. Easier to talk down (Deception/Persuasion DC 13).
- Brian's union background helps: he knows industrial site layouts. Advantage on navigation and finding the office.

**Non-combat option:** Dag can get them in during a delivery. They ride in his truck, have 15 minutes while he distracts the guard. Clean. No combat. But Dag is now involved (leverage if caught).

**Rewards:** Transport log showing 43-mile NE trip on Lena's date. Vehicle ID numbers. Pattern of regular supply runs to the same destination.

### 4. LAUNDROMAT AMBUSH -- Pointview Industrial
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Trigger:** Meeting Free X contacts. Someone followed them -- C&S enforcers, or DPA.
**Location:** Laundromat in Pointview Industrial. Night. Washers humming. Fluorescent buzz. Soap smell. Back alley exit.

**Enemies (Option A -- C&S):** 2 C&S Enforcers + 1 driver waiting outside (same stats, SMG only)
**Enemies (Option B -- DPA):** 2 DPA Field Agents + 1 DPA Senior Agent

**Setup:** The PCs are inside talking to the Free X Lady (or Salo). A black sedan pulls up. Two figures get out. The Free X Lady goes pale: "They followed you. Or me. Back door. NOW."

**Complications:**
- The Free X Lady / Salo must escape. If captured, that thread goes cold for weeks.
- The laundromat has industrial washers (full cover), a narrow back hallway (single file), and a back door to the alley.
- Alley leads to a chain-link fence (Athletics DC 12 to climb) or a fire escape to the roof.
- One washer is running. Push an enemy into it = improvised attack (MUS DC 13, 1d8 bludgeoning + soaked). Creative Surge opportunity.
- If C&S: they want to capture and interrogate. FEAR pistols to break morale.
- If DPA: they want to arrest. "DPA! On the ground! Hands where I can see them!"
- The Free X Lady knows a drain grate in the back alley that connects to storm tunnels. DC 14 MUS to pry open. Escape route, but dark and tight.

**Rewards:** Free X trust increases if PCs protect their people. If C&S: confirms The Chairman has eyes on them (Murph spotlight). If DPA: confirms they're being actively tracked.

### 5. CAMPUS INCIDENT -- P.I.P.S.
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard (social + optional combat)
**Trigger:** Agent Moss escalates. DPA sends a team to P.I.P.S. to question the boys.
**Location:** P.I.P.S. campus. Courtyard, Alan's office, hallways. Daytime. Students everywhere.

**Enemies:** 1 DPA Senior Agent (Moss or replacement) + 2 DPA Field Agents. NOT tactical -- this is a public institution. Suits, badges, clipboards.

**Setup:** Three agents arrive at the front office. They have a "voluntary interview request" for Brian McKnight regarding "a grid irregularity in The Sevens." Not an arrest. Not yet. But the pressure is real.

**Complications:**
- Alan intercepts. "My students are under my academic supervision. You'll interview them in my office, with me present." The DPA can't refuse without a warrant.
- In the interview: Deception vs Insight skill challenge. Brian needs 3 successes before 2 failures. Other PCs can assist (Help action for advantage) if they're present and have a reason to be.
  - Agent Insight: +5 (Senior), +3 (Field)
  - DCs: Deception DC 15, Persuasion DC 13, Intimidation DC 17 (risky -- shows aggression)
- If Brian fails: they don't arrest him, but his name goes on a formal investigation file. +1d4 Heat. Moss comes back with a warrant next time.
- If Brian succeeds: the agents leave unsatisfied. Moss suspects but has nothing. She'll try another angle (Mrs. Chen, Uffe, traffic cameras).
- If the PCs panic and run: it becomes a chase. 3 rounds of pursuit through campus. Moss calls for tactical backup. This gets very bad very fast. +2d6 Heat.
- Alan's agenda: he's helpful but he's watching. If the PCs reveal too much about Grimsson's research in front of him, he'll press harder for the device.

**Combat trigger:** Only if the PCs start it. The DPA won't draw weapons on a school campus unless fired upon. If combat happens: every student with a clicker knows. +3d6 Heat. National news.

**Rewards:** If handled clean: proof they can outmaneuver the system. Edge: +2 to Deception vs DPA (they rehearsed it). If Alan helps: he calls in the favor later.

### 6. SALO'S TRAILER -- Pointview Industrial
**Difficulty:** Easy-Medium
**Trigger:** Finding Salo. Someone else wants him quiet.
**Location:** Scrapyard, Pointview Industrial. Night. Salo's trailer behind stacks of crushed cars. One light on inside. Dog barking somewhere.

**Enemies:** 3 Street Toughs (hired to intimidate Salo) + 1 C&S Enforcer (handler, stays in car)

**Setup:** The PCs arrive to find three guys hanging around Salo's trailer. One's leaning on the door. They were paid to scare him into keeping quiet -- someone at C&S learned Salo exists. They're not killers. They're $200 and a threat.

**Complications:**
- The toughs fold fast (morale DC 12 after first goes down). The enforcer in the car is the real problem -- he's on the phone to C&S. If not stopped, he reports the PCs' descriptions.
- Salo is inside, drunk, scared. He heard them arrive and is clutching a wrench. He'll swing at anyone who opens the door without announcing themselves (surprise attack, +2, 1d6+1).
- If the PCs handle the toughs WITHOUT violence (Intimidation DC 13 or Persuasion DC 15): Salo trusts them more. He saw through the window. "You didn't hit them. Good. I'm tired of people who hit first."
- If violence: Salo still talks, but he's more guarded. Fewer details.

**Rewards:** Salo's intel (facility layout, Lena's room). Trust from Salo if handled well. The enforcer's phone (if captured) has C&S contact numbers -- breadcrumb to The Chairman's operation.

### 7. FIRE ROAD -- Route 9, Northeast
**Difficulty:** Hard-Deadly
**Trigger:** The PCs follow Renko's trail toward the facility. Late campaign encounter.
**Location:** Fire road off Route 9. Pine forest. Gravel. Chain-link gate with a forestry service sign. Dusk.

**Enemies:** 2 DPA Tactical + 1 DPA Senior Agent (checkpoint)

**Setup:** A concealed checkpoint 2 miles down the fire road. Tire spikes under gravel (Perception DC 16 to spot from the vehicle). If they trip the spikes, the tactical team emerges from tree cover. The senior agent steps out with hands visible: "You're on federal land. Turn around."

**Complications:**
- The tactical team has assault rifles with PENETRATING. Cover doesn't help as much.
- The senior agent gives ONE chance to leave. "No paperwork if you go now." This is real -- she'd rather they leave than file an incident report this close to the facility.
- If combat: the tactical team flanks (they know the terrain). One goes high (ridge), one goes low (drainage ditch). Senior agent provides NEURAL LOCK support.
- Hera (the van) is disabled if they hit the tire spikes. Max can try to get her moving (WHEELS DC 16, THAUMATECH DC 14 to jury-rig).
- Retreat is always available. They can leave. This confirms the facility exists and is guarded. That's intel.
- If they somehow get past: the gravel road descends into a clearing. Generator compound. A building that looks like a forestry service station. And a ramp going down.

**Rewards:** Visual confirmation of the facility entrance. If they capture or talk down an agent: confirmation that "Thaumic Research & Containment" is the official designation. The tactical team's radio frequency (THAUMATECH DC 14 to pull from a dropped COMM-SET).
