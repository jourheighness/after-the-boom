# SESSION LOG: 003

**Date (in-world):** Early December, 1992 (a few days after Session 01)
**Incident:** The Red Thread / Grimsson's Research
**Location:** P.I.P.S., The Sevens (Block 7), Ulfsson Repairs

---

## Summary of Events

### Morning: Classes at P.I.P.S.
- **Thaumaturgic Law (Oliver & Murph):** Prof. Emmery, exhausted, played a video lecture. Sarah De La Coeur approached Oliver about his band; he let her down gently. Murph left class to research Covenant & Sterling in the library.
- **Murph's Library Research:** C&S is one of the Big 10 — unusual for a firm started one year after The Boom. Specialization: Mergers & Acquisitions. Connected to thaumic pharmacology. Potential acquisition target: Pax Life Pharmaceuticals (Murph's father's company).
- **Workplace Safety (Brian):** Instructor Brutus (Steel & Tusk). Brian frustrated — too much talk, not enough action. Felt Brutus was appealing to corporations instead of forcing change.
- **Alternative Medicine (Max):** Silver Moonbeam taught energizing extracts. Max skipped to skateboard with Brent.

### Professor Alan Intercepts
Needed someone to check on Mr. Grimsson — a dwarf conspiracy theorist who'd gone dark. Claimed to have info on thaumic positives and the DPA. The group accepted.

### The Drive
Took the P.I.P.S. van (Hera, Contained Elemental motor, loves birch tinder). Max knows how to get it going from his father at Ulfsson Repairs. Drove to The Sevens.

### Grimsson's Apartment
Found a scrying device — attempt to locate disappeared positives. The Free X Lady entered: former member of Free X, knew Grimsson. Oliver recognized Free X from the punk scene. Found news clippings, blueprints, evidence of DPA disappearances. She gave them a key to Grimsson's cellar.

### The Cellar: The Red Thread
False wall. Motherlode of Grimsson's research. He'd USED the scrying device. **THE REVELATION:** Grimsson found Lena Ulfsson — Max's mother. Polaroid captured from a mirror showing Lena looking directly back. She's alive. She saw him looking. Max was shocked.

### DPA Arrives
Agents appeared while they were in the cellar. Grabbed everything: scrying device, research papers, experimental diary, documentation, polaroid.

**The Chase:** DPA pursued through the building. Brian sabotaged the thaumagrid in an alley — electrical arc jumped to DPA vehicle, crashed them. Team escaped.

### The Getaway
Pulled over en route to Ulfsson Repairs. Officer Karl let them go (Murph talked). Arrived safely. Max struggled to process the discovery. Resolution: they're going to find Lena.

### The Chairman's Follow-Up
Murph's pager buzzed. Instructions to add what he knows to an envelope at home. The debt rises. Murph faces: what does he tell C&S about Lena?
