# Campaign: The Rig
*Slow-burn psychological horror on an offshore research platform.*

## Premise
Two foster brothers on Meridian Station. Normal onboarding, then things get wrong. Three factions with competing agendas. An ancient entity studying them through their dreams. Nobody gets out clean.

## Tone
Industrial horror. Not fantasy horror. The monster is alien, but the human choices are what matter. Think *The Thing* meets *Annihilation* meets corporate negligence.

## The Factions
- **OTRE** — Scientists. Want to understand dimensional bleed. Brought Brenor.
- **JSOT** — Military. Want weaponization. Led by Holt. "Can you put a leash on an extraplanar entity?"
- **C&S** — Saboteurs. The Chairman wants this research dead. Dimensional access threatens the infernal business model. Method: Jack Miller, unknowing asset.

## The Aboleth
Ancient. Patient. Has been sending dreams to the crew for weeks. Not nightmares — memories. Its memories. Learning how humans work. Preparing. The breach won't be an accident. The aboleth has been shaping events, influencing key people, ensuring the barrier fails at exactly the right moment.

**What it wants:** Through. Worship. The leyline. Billions of minds to touch.

## Session Structure
1. **Arrival & Onboarding** — Safe. Professional. Industrial. Plant wrongness subtly.
2. **The First Night** — Personal wrongness. Strange dreams. Something off, probably nothing.
3. **Escalation** — Something IS wrong. People notice. JSOT pushes harder.
4. **The Break** — Containment fails. Paranoia. Who's real? Spaces don't connect right.
5. **The Manifestation** — Survival horror. The monster is here.
6. **Resolution** — Costly choices: Escape / Containment / Destruction / Something Worse.

## Campaign State (Post-Session 1)
The brothers arrived, met everyone, got onboarded. Jack became second-in-command after handling Mike. JSOT arrived with new hardware — they want to push more energy into the leyline anomaly. Both brothers dreamed (cold water, pressure, vast presence). Day two brought clicker inconsistencies — different readings on different devices. Riley's clicker showed 0.0 while Jack found the security office empty with a lit cigarette.

They reached the research area to find JSOT machines running impossible numbers, early warning systems sabotaged. Riley turned around — white eyes, rictus smile. "It's coming." She pressed EXECUTE. Reality bending.

## Secrets & Clues
1. Dreams started three weeks before PCs arrived. Everyone's having them. No one's talking.
2. Park's notes show he volunteered to touch the breach.
3. Holt's encrypted comms reference "acceptable losses" and "capture protocol."
4. Wrench found evidence of sabotage on containment array.
5. Jack's security clearance is higher than it should be. Someone upgraded it.
6. The aboleth has been in contact with someone on the mainland.
7. C&S internal memo (on Jack's hidden comm unit): "Confirm asset in place. Await trigger phrase."
8. Brenor's convergence maps — the aboleth has been influencing leyline formation for *centuries*.
