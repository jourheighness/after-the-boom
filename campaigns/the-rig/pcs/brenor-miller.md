# Brenor Miller
**Dwarf (Displaced) — Ranger (Field Researcher)**

## Quick Profile
Wiry, slim for a dwarf. Brown beard, more hair than grooming sense. Looks like someone who spent too many nights in university libraries. Carries himself like he's used to being underestimated — and used to proving people wrong.

## Background
Came through with The Boom as an infant, no memory of Flone. Foster system. Grew up with Jack Miller. Education became his way out — and he was *good* at it.

Doctorate from **Beaumont State College** in thauma-geology. One of the few Displaced-majority institutions in the FU, still underfunded, still fighting for recognition.

### The Work
**Specialty:** Thauma-geology and leyline studies. Where magic flows and why the earth shaped it that way. Pre-Boom geological formations that seem to have anticipated leyline paths.

**The Discovery:** He's onto something. Years of data, dozens of sites, a pattern emerging — leyline convergence points and ancient geological events. Something that might rewrite what the FU thinks about where magic comes from.

**The Problem:** Can't get funding. Robert Grease (tenured mentor) stole his breakthroughs, published under his own name.

### The Recruitment
Post-doc at **Halrow University, Motor City**. Respectable work, dead-end trajectory. Then the black car. They knew his work. One year of funding, fully resourced. No university politics. They showed him a folder of research that dovetailed perfectly with his own discoveries. One name: **Dr. Meredith Vosslin.** "She asked for you personally."

**What Brenor doesn't know:** The aboleth has been influencing leyline formation for *centuries*. He's been studying its footprint without knowing it.

## The Keepsake
Thick silver bracelet, worn smooth. Old dwarven runes — something about mountains, stone, depth, endurance. Almost a psalm. His true name is inscribed inside. He doesn't know who put it on his wrist or what clan it marks. The only proof someone wanted him to remember.

## The Miller Family
Same as Jack's file. Joe & Betty Miller, Beaumont. Stable, warm, hand-me-down clothes.

## What Drives Him
The research. Always the research. Everything else fades when he's in the field, reading the stone, tracing the lines. He wants recognition, but more than that he wants to *know*. He's close. He can feel it.

## What Haunts Him
- The foster system. Years of being nobody's priority.
- Robert Grease. The way the system rewards theft if you're tenured.
- The bracelet and the family he'll never know.
- The suspicion that even this job is using him for something he doesn't understand.

## Session 1 State
Celebrity among the researchers. Validated by the leyline data. Talked to Wrench about concerns with JSOT pushing harder. Witnessed Riley compromised. In the research area as reality bends. The clickers showed impossible numbers.
