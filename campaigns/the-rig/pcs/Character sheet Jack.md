# Jack Miller

**Background**  
“Never had Brenor's head for books. Had other talents — speed, strength, the ability to read a room and know who mattered. Military was the obvious path. Signed up at eighteen. Found a home.”

**Army sniper, two tours.** Sat still for hours in bad weather and worse terrain, waited, made the shot, and came home different.  
**Boon domain:** long-range shooting, patience and observation, terrain reading.  
**Example uses:** finding a firing position in an unfamiliar building, estimating distance and wind, staying motionless when every instinct says move.
