# Jack Miller
**Half-Elf (Displaced) — Fighter (Soldier)**

## Quick Profile
Tall, broad, jarhead build. Human-passing at a glance — round ears, strong jaw. But the cheekbones are too sharp, light catches his eyes wrong. Handsome in a bland, institutional way. The face of someone who learned early that fitting in was survival.

## Background
Never had Brenor's head for books. Had speed, strength, the ability to read a room. Classic jock, popular. Military was the obvious path. Signed up at eighteen. Found a home.

### The Sumerian Knot
Two-year deployment. Insurgents and djinn. Sand and leyline interference. Saw some shit. Came back with a Distinguished Service Medal — saved an officer from an ambush. The citation makes it sound heroic; Jack knows it was instinct and luck.

### The Corporate Slide
Came home, found work at **Schwartz-Zee Private Security**. Then C&S bought the company. Now corporate security with a military pedigree. The money's better. The jobs are different. He doesn't ask too many questions.

### The Recruitment
Approached first. Government contract, high security. Good money. Then they mentioned Brenor. Jack didn't think twice. The fact that C&S wanted it that way — that's just how things work.

**What Jack doesn't know:** He's a C&S asset. He's carrying something. His check-ins go somewhere wrong. He's the failsafe. Brenor was bait — his name "suggested" to OTRE by a C&S front.

## The Miller Family
**Joe & Betty Miller** opened their foster home in Beaumont after the mines closed. Coal workers, third generation. Then CTEs replaced coal. They looked at the Displaced kids flooding the foster system and saw something familiar — people the system had no use for. The Miller home wasn't fancy but it was stable. Still in Beaumont, still taking in kids.

## What Drives Him
- Protecting Brenor. Always. Even when Brenor doesn't want protecting.
- Belonging. The squad. The people who count on him.
- Being good at his job. The one thing he's never doubted.

## What Haunts Him
- The Sumerian Knot. What he did. What he saw. What he didn't stop. The shadow on the horizon. The Great Eye.
- The suspicion he's good at following orders because he's afraid to question them.
- The elf-blood. The thing that marks him as other.
- The growing sense that C&S isn't what it pretends to be.

## The Flaw
Falls for peer pressure. Group cohesion is everything. Popular because he never rocks the boat. Follows orders because the alternative is chaos. A perfect soldier — easy to use if you know how.

## Session 1 State
Now second-in-command of security (Riley promoted him). Made an example of Mike. Dreamed of the Sumerian Knot — the shadow, the machine gun, mowing down his own squad. Witnessed Riley compromised (white eyes, rictus smile, "It's coming"). In the research area as reality bends.
