How It Works

Left: "Tracking"

  • Setup die — value shown, dot raster style. Used in roll dialog.
  • Sustained on enemies — die type shown (d6, d4). Click to roll → posts damage to chat. On 1 → auto-removes. Label = short note ("Grunt #2").
  • + button — add tracked die manually (pick die type, enter label).
  • × to clear — remove any tracked die.

Right: "On You"

  • Sustained on self — die type shown. Click to roll → damage through YOUR pipeline → posts to chat. On 1 → burns out, auto-removes.
  • + button — GM or player adds sustained effect.
  • × to clear — spent Act to end it.

Take Damage

Type a number, hit Apply. The applyDamage() pipeline runs automatically (Armor → Guard → Scar → Harm overflow) and posts a breakdown to chat. Could live in the resources row next to Guard, or as its own strip below die tracking.
