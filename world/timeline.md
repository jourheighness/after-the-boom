# Mondas Timeline

## Era 1: The Wobble (1950–1971)
Physics drifts. Governments notice. The public does not.

- **1950–1960: The Nuclear Fizzle.** FU and CSS stockpile nukes, but yields are inconsistent. MAD is quietly retired in internal memos. "Non-deterministic yield profile." Cold War shifts to massive conventional armies.
- **1962: The Caoba Guidance Crisis.** CSS places missiles in Caoba. FU blockades. Ends when guidance systems return null-solution trajectories — missiles can't be aimed at cities that "don't exist" according to targeting computers. Blamed on gyroscope humidity.
- **1965–1971: The Space Race.** Less about glory, more about escaping a planet that feels wrong. Newtonian mechanics stops working past the ionosphere. Astronauts suffer "The Break" — acute cognitive collapse, Type-Ψ visual intrusions ("The Great Eye"). Moon landing scrubbed. FU pivots to subterranean defense.
- **1968–1971: Project BACKLINE.** Joint FU / Nordic Ward States black-budget program. Abandons nuclear metrics for tracking "the background weird." Creates the BTC scale. Provisional scale circulating in classified annexes by 1971.

## Era 2: The Boom (1972)
- **July 16, 1972.** Global BTC spikes from 0.05 to 4.51 in twelve minutes. The Displacement begins — Displaced arrive worldwide. A sawmill in Fernreach finds dwarves under a conveyor belt. A train near Kragov brakes for an elf on the tracks. An orc cries at a refrigerator in Porto Verde.
- **FU Response:** Declares "multi-national refugee situation." Reopens army bases as Processing Centers. First intake forms include fields for "species (observer assessment)" and "measured sensitivity."
- **CSS Response:** Classifies Displaced as "specialized foreign labor." Contracts they can't read. Assigned to factories.

## Era 3: The Grey Scare & Resource Wars (1973–1979)
- **1973–1976: The Grey Scare.** Panic over CSS necromancy. Tabloids run stories about "Grey Brigades." Congressional hearings on posthumous labor conscription.
- **1973–1975: The Catsang Ley-Conflict.** Proxy war over the Dragon's Vein leyline. FU psychological casualty rates hit 60% in some sectors. The jungle learns. FU withdraws.
- **1976: The Aurum-Null Standard.** Helvetian bankers discover gold exposed to high ley radiation becomes the perfect thaumic insulator. Helvetia becomes the world's Faraday Cage.
- **1977–1979: First Consumer Wave.** CTEs in large buildings. Pilot programs. The clicker enters homes (~two weeks' wages).
- **1979: Death of Oil.** FU perfects the thauma-steam turbine. Crude collapses. The Great Retrofit begins.

## Era 4: The Industrial-Arcano Complex (1980–1989)
- **1980–1985: Mass Adoption.** By 1982 most new FU construction includes a basement CTE. Clickers go mainstream by 1984 (price of a good radio). Siphon grids expand.
- **1982: Integration.** Displaced released from processing centers into civil society. Elves → banking/actuarial. Dwarves → metalwork/construction. Orcs → heavy industry/unions. Brotherhood of Steel and Tusk formed.
- **1983–1988: CSS and Dead Labour.** INPUs (Integrated Necro-Productive Units) established. "Post-Mortem Productive Continuity." The Displaced who are injured get offered "continuity contracts."
- **1986: The Pryrichchia Logic-Collapse.** CSS fracking experiment ruptures a deep leyline. Inside the 30km exclusion zone, agreement breaks. Independent instruments return different readings. Records diverge. The Cover (Conceptual Containment Structure) is built over the rupture. LPI formalized as a standard metric.
- **1985–1989: Second Consumer Wave.** Thaumic appliances (stoves, fridges, washing machines). Licensed personal tools. Thaumic telephone switching. Hospitals adopt thaumic diagnostics.
- **1989: The Spreestadt Stasis.** Der Ring holds. CSS economy stalls but doesn't collapse. Necro-labor and Displaced labor maintain output.

## Era 5: The Proxy Era (1990–1992)
- **1991: War of the Sand-Glass.** FU, CSS, and Nine Provinces back different warlords in Al-ʿUqda (the Sumerian Knot) for ley-siphon rights. First openly modern thaumic war. FU deploys thaumic-hardened tanks and line projectors. CSS fields mobile INPU columns. FU secures primary leylines. Siphon-burn changes the geography permanently.
- **1990–1992: Third Consumer Wave.** Thaumatech normalization. Children born after 1980 have never known a world without CTEs. Prototype thaumic-assisted computers. Personal wands still heavily regulated. Rural areas lag behind.

## 1992: The Present Day
The race is about scaling and normalizing thaumic technology. The world is tired. Permits, forms, safety codes, and the quiet hum of something not quite understood.
