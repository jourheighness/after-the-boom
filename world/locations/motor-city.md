# Motor City (Greater Ostreon Metropolitan Area)
*Post-industrial powerhouse that pivoted from automobiles to thauma-tech.*

## History
**Pre-Boom (1950s–1972):** Manufacturing capital of the FU. Auto industry HQ, union strongholds, assembly lines.

**Post-Boom (1972–1992):** Massive pivot. Car factories → CTE fabrication plants. Assembly lines retooled for thaumic capacitors, mark-weapons, ward arrays, industrial magical infrastructure.

## Major Corporations
- **Union Electric & Spellworks (UE&S)** — Energy giant, HQ in Core City
- **Pax Life Sciences** — Pharmaceutical megacorp. Produces Stabil-Tabs. Murph's father is a higher-up.
- **Covenant & Sterling LLC** — "Consulting firm." Actually infernal contract facilitation. One of the Big 10, founded suspiciously soon after The Boom. The Chairman's front.

## Districts

### Core City (Downtown)
Gleaming financial district. Glass towers. Where deals happen. C&S offices in a bland mid-rise.

### Bloomfield Hills
Old money suburb, 20 min north. Pre-Boom wealth → post-Boom markets. Mansions, private schools. Moore family (Murph's father's side) has roots here.

### Oakview
See separate file. 40 min north. Blue-collar, P.I.P.S.

### Black Creek Industrial Corridor
CTE generation facilities, UE&S plants, thaumic capacitor factories. Runs between Motor City and Oakview. High BTC, dangerous work.

## The Reality in 1992
Motor City works. The machines run. But:
- Industrial zones have high HRI (decades of thaumic emissions)
- Workers burn out young
- Pharmaceutical dependency epidemic (Stabil-Tabs)
- Wealth concentrated in Core City and Bloomfield Hills
- Rust-belt towns like Oakview bear the cost

**The slogan:** "Motor City: Powering the Union's Future"
**The reality:** Same extraction, same exploitation — just with fire elementals instead of internal combustion.
