# Meridian Station (OTRE Platform 7 / "The Edge")
*Offshore research rig over a leyline convergence in the North Atlantic.*

## Overview
Old FU leyline extraction platform. Decommissioned after Pryrichchia (1986) made direct-tap operations politically toxic. Mothballed, forgotten, then quietly converted for OTRE experimental research.

Sits over a leyline convergence point — exactly the kind of formation Brenor's been studying. The barrier between here and *elsewhere* is thin. Things bleed through. OTRE has been studying the bleed. They don't know something has been studying them back.

## Project Codename
**ATLANTIC MARGIN**

## Factions on the Rig

### OTRE (Office of Thaumic Research and Extraction)
The scientists. Civilian agency under DPA. Officially: improving siphon efficiency, leyline mapping. Unofficially: studying dimensional bleed that could revolutionize energy. Brought in Brenor for his convergence expertise.

### JSOT (Joint Special Operations, Thaumic)
The military. Born from the Sumerian Knot — when you fight djinn, you start wondering if you could *have* djinn. Led by Major David Holt. Want weaponization: can extraplanar entities be controlled?

### Covenant & Sterling
The saboteur. The Chairman does NOT want this research to succeed. Dimensional access threatens the infernal business model. Method: Jack Miller, unknowing C&S asset. He's carrying something. His check-ins go somewhere wrong.

## The Aboleth
No pronounceable name. Waiting between dimensions longer than human civilization. When the rig started tapping the leyline, it noticed. It's been:
- Sending dreams (memories of oceanic depths, ancient cities, worship)
- Learning every mind it touches
- Preparing — shaping events so the breach happens at exactly the right moment

By the time it manifests, it's already been inside their heads. The dreams weren't warnings — they were reconnaissance.
