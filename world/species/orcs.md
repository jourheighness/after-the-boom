# Orcs (Displaced)

## FU Integration
Physically robust, high tolerance for environmental stress. Concentrated in heavy industry, logistics, hazardous-environment work. Longshoremen, warehouse logistics, freight. Quickly became influential in unions handling high-BTC labor. The Brotherhood of Steel and Tusk (orc-dwarf alliance) became a major political force. Slogans stay mundane: better boots, better masks, shorter shifts.

## Culture
Recreated clan bonds through labor solidarity. "My local" replaced "my clan." Union wages good — orcs doing better than many Displaced.

## Discrimination
Physical stereotyping: "good for heavy lifting," dangerous when angry. Feared more than respected. Face the most overt racism of the Displaced. Police interactions worse. Corporate hiring rare.

## Extraction Angle
Very high capacity. Robust recovery. Targeted for the most dangerous thaumic work — siphon maintenance, wild elemental capture, Caution/Danger zone labor. Spark Wage partially compensates but doesn't cover long-term health effects.
