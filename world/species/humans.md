# Humans

## The Default
Humans didn't arrive during The Boom — they were already here. They're the ones who built the systems, wrote the laws, and decided how magic would be regulated. For better and worse.

## Discrimination Pattern
None aimed at them — they're doing the discriminating. Economic class matters more than species for humans. A poor human in The Sevens has more in common with a displaced dwarf neighbor than with a Bloomfield Hills executive.

## Extraction Angle
Lost nuclear certainty, gained magic through EXTRACTION. Built economy on treating people (including themselves) as batteries. The Displaced integrated into a system designed to extract value from human labor — then the system learned it could extract even more from non-human labor.

## Thaumic Status
Some humans are Thaumic Positive (Sparkers). Most are Zeroes. The ratio varies by region but roughly 1 in 8 humans test positive by age 16.
