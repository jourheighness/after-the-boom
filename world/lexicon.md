# The Lexicon of Mondas

## Thaumo-Metric Tripod
- **BTC (Background Thaumic Count):** Raw density of ambient thaumic activity. Measured by clickers. Developed in Project BACKLINE during the Wobble.
- **LPI (Local Paradox Index):** Dimensionless score for how likely small contradictions are to *persist*. Zones >3.0 require redundant logging and memory-audits. Formalized after the 1986 Pryrichchia Logic-Collapse.
- **HRI (Haunt Risk Index):** Actuarial measure of spontaneous ghost formation per 10,000 deaths. Used by hospitals and insurers.

**Safety Scale:** Safe (0.0–2.0) | Caution (2.1–5.0) | Danger (5.0+, Spark Wage mandatory)

## Eras & History
- **The Wobble (1950–1971):** Pre-Boom. Physics drifted, nukes fizzled, Space Race ended in "The Break."
- **The Boom (1972):** Global BTC spiked to 4.51. The Displaced arrived.
- **The Grey Scare (1973–1976):** Panic over CSS necromancy.
- **The Resource Wars (1979):** Oil collapsed. World pivoted to CTEs and siphons.
- **Pryrichchia Logic-Collapse (1986):** Failed CSS ley-fracking created "The Zone."
- **War of the Sand-Glass (1991):** FU won the Sumerian Knot using siphons.

## Factions & Geopolitics
- **Federal Union (FU):** "Flash Dominance." High-output magic, consumer convenience, extraction.
- **Confederation of Socialistic States (CSS):** "Labour Beyond Death." Necro-labor. INPUs.
- **Continental Concordat:** "Stability by Design." Harmonics. Albion (finance), Rheinmark (safety), Lumeria (culture).
- **Nine Provinces:** "Harmonious Development." Landscape alchemy. Weather belts. Sacrifice Zones.
- **The Free Marches:** Buffer states. Salvage economies, laundered necro-tech.
- **Helvetia:** The Null-Cantons. Neutral. Aurum-null vaults in the Null Peaks.

## Technology & Infrastructure
- **CTE (Contained Thermal Entity):** Bound elemental for building-scale heat/power. Class ratings CTE-1A through CTE-4C. "Boiler spirits." Need feeding, can be overworked to death.
- **Siphon:** Federal facility extracting raw thaumic energy from leyline bleed-off. Grid-scale. Exclusion zones, armed guards. Also "Thaumic Extraction Units (TEUs)."
- **Siphon-burn:** Causality scarring from thaumic over-extraction. Permanent anomalies: strange weather, drifting geography.
- **INPU (Integrated Necro-Productive Unit):** CSS factory staffed by stabilized corpses. "The Overtime Crew" / "the quiet ones" / "dredges."
- **Aurum-null (EN-71-A):** Magic-proof gold. Perfect thaumic insulator. Helvetia's main export.
- **Surge Grid:** Electrical infrastructure built to tolerate massive short-term magic spikes ("Pops").
- **Clicker:** Handheld BTC reader. Portable thaumic dosimeter. Every household has one by the door.
- **Etching:** Patterned conductive/thaumic layer laid into metal, ceramic, or glass for controlled BTC interaction.
- **Mark:** FU term for a standardized personal etching keyed to an individual's resonance. Used on weapons, tools, equipment.
- **Contact Plate:** Exposed piece of an etching/mark for direct skin contact to complete the circuit.
- **Personal Channel:** Pathway along which a Positive's excess potential is drained into a marked device. Overuse → Static Itch → Burnout.

## Sociology & Slang
- **The Displaced:** Fantasy races (Elves, Dwarves, Orcs, Halflings, Goblins) who arrived during The Boom. "When they showed up."
- **Flone:** The other world/dimension the Displaced came from. Memories of it are fog.
- **Spark Wage:** Hazard pay for high-BTC / Caution zone work.
- **Dead Labour:** Work by reanimated corpses. CSS: "Post-Mortem Productive Continuity."
- **Blue-out:** Localized thaumic exhaustion. Reality stops conducting. Eerie silence, cold.
- **Pop:** Catastrophic surge failure in a device or grid.
- **Static:** Anxiety/tension a Sparker feels when they haven't cast in too long.
- **Grounded:** Chemically suppressing one's magic (e.g., using Blockers / Stabil-Tabs).
- **Being "Written Out":** CSS fear of having one's soul erased from the state labor database.
- **Hero of Development:** Sarcastic term for someone in a Nine Provinces Sacrifice Zone.
- **Thaumic Positive / Sparker:** Born with magical ability. Diagnosed via clicker test ~age 10.
- **Zeroes / Blanks:** Non-magical humans.
- **Yellow Slip:** DPA notification to parents when a child tests Positive.
- **Operator's License:** Laminated card, Classes 1–5, for legal public spellcasting.
- **Burnout:** Degenerative over-channeling condition. Nosebleeds, memory loss, turning into salt.
- **Contractor (Warlock):** Bypassed education by signing a deal with a Patron for power in exchange for KPIs.

## Thaumo-Tech & Networking
- **Weave-Wide Web (WWW):** Global thauma-tech data lattice riding existing copper, crystal, and carrier infrastructure.
- **The Mesh:** Dense local thauma-tech links in cities.
- **ThaumaNet:** Formal Concordat term for the international lattice.
- **The Thread:** Working-class slang for local web access. "The Thread's dead" = outage.
- **ArcaVistae:** Concordat search index. "Ask, and We Catalogue." Sometimes surfaces what you *meant*.
- **Yah-Host:** Albion venture-circle portal. Free ThaumaMail, ads, horoscope stock tips.
- **ScryFind:** Nine Provinces search engine. Pattern-analysis + state-approved guidance.
- **Ask Mort:** CSS Q&A service. Reliable, glacially slow.
- **ThaumaMail:** Routed sealed correspondence over thauma-tech lines.
- **Line Ghosts:** Residual echoes on overused channels. Old conversations replaying over new calls.
- **Lag Fog:** Mental static/nausea from overloaded lines.

## Consumer Products (Oakview, early 1990s)
- **Stabil-Tabs:** Pax Life Sciences suppressant. "Smooths out" excess resonance. Strongly habit-forming. Blunts affect.
- **Spark-Up!:** OTC energy chew. Trace BTC-active carriers. Blamed for nosebleeds and jitters.
- **Null-Nite PM:** Sleep aid for swing-shift workers. Low-dose dampeners. Heavy morning Static.
- **Redline Tonic:** Red sugary soda. "Vitamin-fortified energy" / poor student's healing potion.
- **Patch-It Panacea:** OTC adhesive plasters with slow-release BAF compounds. In every first-aid kit.
- **Fuse-Gum:** Sour chewing gum. Mild stimulants + BTC-tuned sweeteners. "Keeps you synced."
- **Ley-Lite Sports Drink:** Neon-green electrolyte beverage. "Recharges without overcharging the air."

## Military & Crisis
- **Type-Ψ Intrusions:** Official classification for astronaut "visual intrusions" (The Great Eye).
- **Meat-Wave:** FU briefing slang for massed first assault with expendable assets.
- **Dredges:** Front-line nickname for INPUs.
- **Bonded Crew:** Vehicle crew permanently fused into control systems. No hatches, no exit.
- **Heavy:** Any armored platform with a Bonded Crew.
- **Tap Out:** Hit personal channeling limits. Status call when a Positive can't safely fire.
- **Clarity Tabs:** Military-issue perception sharpeners. Severe side effects.

## Locations
- **Oakview:** Rust-belt town in North Ostreon. Home to P.I.P.S. Gray skies, leyline under I-75.
- **P.I.P.S.:** Polytechnic Institute of Paranatural Studies. Brutalist community college for vocational magic.
- **The Zone:** 30km exclusion zone around Pryrichchia. Reality is broken.
- **The Sumerian Knot (Al-ʿUqda):** Scarred landscape of wild magic and siphons.
- **Meridian Station (OTRE Platform 7):** Offshore rig over a leyline convergence in the North Atlantic.

## Infrastructure & Daily Life
- **Elemental Exchange:** Former gas stations converted for CTE fuel trade. Coal, wood, "appeasement charms."
- **Abjuration-glass:** Transparent, bulletproof, soundproof glass. Used in Der Ring (Spreestadt wall).
- **Ghost Routes:** CSS rail lines maintained solely by spirit-bound inspectors.
- **Memorial-Industrial Zone:** CSS zoning combining cemeteries with power substations.
- **VTI (Volatile Temper Index):** Metric on heating bills for how angry the building's boiler spirit is.
