# Mondas: World Overview

Mondas is an alternate Earth. Same continents, same basic history up to the mid-20th century — then everything went sideways.

## The Elevator Pitch
It's 1992. Twenty years ago, magic flooded the world. Fantasy races appeared with no memory of where they came from. Now magic is a regulated utility, boiler spirits heat your apartment, and your government-issued clicker tells you if the air is safe. The Cold War didn't end — it just got weirder. You live in a rust-belt town where the real monsters wear suits and carry clipboards.

## The Boom (1972)
On July 16, 1972, the global Background Thaumic Count (BTC) spiked from 0.05 to 4.51 in twelve minutes. Nobody knows why. The "background" of reality changed permanently.

**What happened:**
- Magic became real and measurable
- The Displaced appeared — elves, dwarves, orcs, goblins, halflings — in random locations worldwide, with skills but no memories of their homeland (called "Flone" by lorekeepers)
- Nuclear weapons became unreliable (they'd been fizzling since the 1950s — "the Wobble")
- Oil collapsed by 1979, replaced by thaumic energy (CTEs and siphons)

## The Major Powers (1992)

### Federal Union (FU)
The superpower. Alt-USA. Doctrine: **"Flash Dominance"** — high-output, fast-surge magic. Consumer convenience and industrial extraction. Obsessed with output curves. Declared the Displaced a "multi-national refugee situation" and built Processing Centers. Now the most integrated society on Mondas, but the integration runs on exploitation. The DPA (Department of Paranatural Affairs) handles registration, training, and disappearances.

### Confederation of Socialistic States (CSS)
Alt-USSR. Doctrine: **"Labour Beyond Death."** Uses necro-labor — reanimated corpses (INPUs) performing repetitive tasks under "Post-Mortem Productive Continuity." The Displaced are classified as "specialized foreign labour." The economy is stalling but not collapsing. Spreestadt (alt-Berlin) is divided by Der Ring — abjuration-glass wall, guard posts, monitoring stations.

### Continental Concordat
Alt-EU. Doctrine: **"Stability by Design."** The bureaucrats of magic. Focuses on Harmonics — keeping reality from tearing. Member states: Albion (alt-UK, finance), Rheinmark (alt-Germany, safety engineering), Lumeria (alt-France, culture). Uses Form 27-exceptions to import banned CSS necro-parts.

### Nine Provinces
Alt-China. Doctrine: **"Harmonious Development."** Massive geo-engineering: ley-guided rivers, weather belts, tiered agro-zones. Treats land, factories, and citizens as matter to be transmuted. Sacrifice Zones for dangerous experiments. From orbit, their projects look like neat rectangles.

### Helvetia
Alt-Switzerland. **The world's Faraday Cage.** Neutral. Protected by massive stockpiles of aurum-null (magic-proof gold). BTC reads 0.00 within its borders — "The Quiet." Remote viewing and divination fail at the border. "The Blind Spot."

### Nordic Ward States
Wardmakers, fault-tenders, and the people who put fences around holes in the world.

### The Free Marches
Buffer states between Concordat and CSS. Salvage economies running on rebuilt/laundered necro-tech.

## Magic System (How It Works)
Magic is measured, regulated, and mundane. See `lexicon.md` for full terminology.

**The Thaumo-Metric Tripod:**
- **BTC** (Background Thaumic Count) — ambient thaumic density. The "Geiger counter" reading.
- **LPI** (Local Paradox Index) — how likely small contradictions are to *persist*. Keys move. Records rewrite. Above 3.0 requires redundant logging.
- **HRI** (Haunt Risk Index) — spontaneous ghost formation per 10,000 deaths. Used by hospitals and insurers.

**Safety Scale:** Safe (0–2.0) → Caution (2.1–5.0) → Danger (5.0+, "Wild Magic," Spark Wage mandatory)

**Personal Magic:**
- **Thaumic Positive** ("Sparker") — born with magical ability. Diagnosed via clicker test ~age 10. Yellow Slip sent to parents. Mandatory DPA registration by 16.
- **Zeroes** — non-magical humans. Zero on the BTC.
- **Static** — the anxiety/tension a Sparker feels when they haven't cast in too long.
- **Burnout** — degenerative condition from over-channeling. Nosebleeds, memory loss, turning into salt.

**Energy Infrastructure:**
- **CTEs** (Contained Thermal Entities) — bound elementals in building basements. The "coal plant" of magic. Need feeding, can be overworked to death. Building-scale.
- **Siphons** — federal facilities tapping leyline bleed-off. The "nuclear plant" of magic. Exclusion zones, armed guards. Grid-scale.

## The Displaced
Fantasy races who arrived during The Boom. Called "Displaced" officially, "when they showed up" colloquially. Remember skills but not history. Their homeland is called **Flone** by lorekeepers.

See `species/` for detailed profiles.

## The Mood
The world is tired. The Boom promised wonders. It delivered permits, forms, safety codes, and the quiet hum of something not quite understood. Twenty years later, most people just want their radiators to work and their clickers to stay in the green.
