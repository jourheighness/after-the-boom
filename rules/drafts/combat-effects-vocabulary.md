# Combat Effects Vocabulary

9 base effects. Everything a gambit can produce. Character-specific (Edges) and equipment-specific (weapon properties, thaumatech) effects extend this list but don't replace it.

## Enemy Debuffs (4)

| Effect | Mechanic | Duration |
|-|-|-|
| **Impaired** | -2 damage dealt | One turn |
| **Exposed** | +2 damage taken | One turn |
| **Prone** | Stand costs Move. Melee Boon / Ranged Snag against them. | Until they stand |
| **Pinned** | Can't reposition. Act to escape. | Until they escape |

## Player Buffs (3)

| Effect | Mechanic | Duration |
|-|-|-|
| **Banked Die** | Set aside a die (keep value). Add to any roll or spend on a gambit. One max. | Until used or combat ends |
| **Armor** | +1 or +2 flat damage reduction | Until you move |
| **Detail** | Learn something hidden | Instant |

## Positioning (2)

| Effect | Mechanic | Duration |
|-|-|-|
| **Push** | Force one band away | Instant |
| **Pull** | Force one band toward | Instant |
