# Archetypes Reference Card

## Core Principles

1. **No classes.** Characters are built from stats + Edges + Background. Identity emerges from combinations, not labels.
2. **Stats are permanent.** 6 points across Grit/Sharp/Nerve (min 1 each, max 4). Never change after creation. Growth is lateral.
3. **Asymmetric balance.** Zeroes get 4 Edges + Thaumic Null. Positives get 3 Edges + thaumic access. Different strengths, not better/worse.
4. **Progression is earned, not tracked.** ~1 Edge per 2-3 sessions from character-defining moments. No XP. No levels. Guard grows from Scars.
5. **Background is narrative identity.** One-sentence expertise gives Boon domain. If the Boon would apply >50% of rolls, narrow the scope.

## Key Decisions (locked)

- Stats: Grit, Sharp, Nerve. 6 points, min 1 each, max 4. Fixed at creation (D18)
- Guard: 2 + highest stat (D17, D33)
- Species: purely narrative, no stat bonuses (D19)
- Zero/positive balance: 4 Edges + Thaumic Null vs 3 Edges + magic access (D20)
- Thaumic Null: invisible to thaumic detection, BTC scans, wards, targeting. Baseline trait, not Edge (D23)
- Contact plates: personal thaumatech requires positive operator (D22)
- No XP, no levels. Edge progression through play. Guard growth from Scars (D21)

## P.I.P.S. Archetypes (reference, not templates)

These four PCs show how stats + Edges produce distinct identities. They are NOT archetypes to copy. They are proof that the system works.

| PC | Stats | Thaumic | Edge Clusters | Role |
|-|-|-|-|-|
| Murph | Nerve-heavy | Shaper (Contractor) | Patron Bond + Operator | Warlock. Corporate pact. KPI-based magic. |
| Brian | Grit-heavy | Knack (Oath) | Oath-Bound + Enforcer | Paladin. Union oath. Physical force. |
| Max | Sharp-heavy | Sensitive (Somatic) | Somatic Discipline (5 Sharp-3 gated) | Monk. Body as channel. Precision. |
| Oliver | Balanced | Knack (Unwitting) | Unwitting Channel + Small Faith + Field Medicine | Cleric. Channels without understanding. |

**Anti-cloning warning:** These PCs share zero Edges. The system produces unique characters when players choose different clusters. If a new character looks like a reskin of one of these four, the Edge selection is too narrow.

## Edge Clusters (13 categories)

**Thaumic identity** (7): Patron Bond, Oath-Bound, Somatic Discipline, Unwitting Channel, Null Field, Hedge Practice, Small Faith

**Professional identity** (7): Investigator, Operator, Technician, Field Medicine, Runner, Enforcer, Handler

**Fringe** (1): Grid Running (leyline hacking)

## Stat Gates

Edges gate behind stats to enforce differentiation:
- **Grit 3**: 8 Edges (physical force, endurance, heavy weapons)
- **Sharp 3**: 11 Edges (precision, reflexes, scanners, Precision Shaping)
- **Nerve 3**: 9 Edges (willpower, influence, shaping bandwidth)

A Grit 3 character and a Sharp 3 character access completely different Edge pools. This is the primary differentiation mechanism after stat assignment.

## Design Space (generalize from these)

- Example A: A displaced elf with Grit 3, zero thaumic potential, 4 Edges in Enforcer + Runner clusters. Street muscle who can't be tracked by wards. Background: "Dockworker foreman, Portview."
- Example B: A human Sensitive with Sharp 3, Somatic Discipline + Investigator clusters. Uses body-reading Tell to detect lies. Background: "Insurance fraud investigator."
- Example C: A dwarf Knack with balanced stats (2/2/2), Hedge Practice + Technician clusters. Fixes thaumatech by feel. Background: "Appliance repair, specializing in thaumic heating."
- Counter-example: "Level 5 Fighter with +3 Strength modifier and Multiattack." Class-level-modifier stack. No room for narrative identity. Stat growth invalidates early choices.
- "Generate unlike these examples. They show range, not targets."

## Boundaries

- Never add stat growth mechanics
- Never create Edges that require specific species
- Never make Zeroes feel like "positives minus magic." They are a distinct play experience.
- Never gate core functionality behind high stats. Stat 1 characters can attempt anything; they just have fewer dice.
- Edge prerequisites max 1 stat gate + 1 Edge prerequisite. No long chains.
