**Key Design Principles:**

- **Common Sense is King.** Rule in the Heroes' favor and keep the story moving.
- **Tactical Action Fantasy.** Quick combat, engaging exploration. Social interactions are handled by roleplay, not gamified.
- **Enemies don't roll.** Heroes must prove their attempts succeed; enemies are assumed to succeed unless Heroes intervene.
- **Order of Operations.** Follow PEMDAS/BODMAS. E.g., halve damage first, then subtract Armor.

## Basics

### The Dice

You need d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, and d20.

- **Cumulative Roll (xd6):** Roll multiple dice and add them together.
- **Exploding Roll (d6!):** If you roll the max value, add another die of the same size. Keep going if it explodes again.
- **Countdown Dice (Cdx):** Roll each Round. On a 1, shrink one die size (e.g., d6 to d4). A 1 on Cd4 ends the countdown.
- **d66 Rolls:** Roll two d6. First is tens, second is ones.

### Stats

Six stats, ranging 2-7: **Might, Dexterity, Awareness, Reason, Presence, Luck.**

| Stat | Used For |
|-|-|
| Might | Endure Saves, Brawl, Melee, Max HP, Inventory Slots |
| Dexterity | Reflex Saves, Finesse, Sneak, Speed |
| Awareness | Detect, Survival, Ranged, Reflex (partial) |
| Reason | Will (partial), Arcana, Craft, Medicine |
| Presence | Will (partial), Influence, Leadership, Performance |
| Luck | Luck Pool (Favor, Fluke) |

### Checks

Roll d20 vs. Difficulty.

- **Crit:** Unmodified 20.
- **Pass:** Equal to or above Difficulty.
- **Fail:** Below Difficulty.

**Difficulty = 20 - (Stat x2 if Trained, otherwise just Stat)**

**Crits on Attack/Cast:** Deal bonus damage equal to the Stat used.
**Crits on Saves:** Unaffected by what provoked the Save; can use an Action.

### Favor & Hinder

- **Favored:** d20 + d6 bonus.
- **Hindered:** d20 - d6 penalty.
- Cancel 1-for-1. Don't stack. Only one d6 rolled either way.

### Hit Points & Damage

- **HP** indicates condition, stamina, ability to avoid death.
- **Damage** is subtracted from HP. Minimum 0 damage.
- **Damage Source:** GM decides if an Ability applies. Describe how you attack (piercing, slashing, etc.).

### Dying

Beings die when:
- HP reduced to 0
- Body destroyed
- 5 Fatigue

Dead Beings can't regain HP. If your Hero dies, make a new one or find a narrative reason they survived.

### Armor / Immune / Weak

- **Armor:** Subtracted from Attack damage.
- **Immune:** Unharmed by that damage.
- **Weak:** Ignores Armor and Immune, deals an extra damage die.

## Adventuring

### Targeting

Anything subjected to an effect is a Target. Must be within the Scene and perceivable. If any part of a Being fits in a targetable area, it is affected.

### Distance

| Term | Measurement |
|-|-|
| Close | Within 5 feet |
| Near | 5-30 feet |
| Far | Beyond 30 feet |

### Vision & Senses

Darkness causes Blinded for those without special senses.

| Sense | Description |
|-|-|
| Allsight | Not fooled by illusions or invisibility |
| Blindsight | Can see even if Blinded |
| Darksight | Can see in darkness |
| Echolocation | Can see by hearing |
| Seismicsense | Can see anything touching ground it touches |
| Telepathy | Can see anything that can think |

### Time

| Term | Approx. | Example |
|-|-|-|
| Round | 10 seconds | Move and take an Action |
| Minute | 6 Rounds | Fight, pick a lock |
| Scene | 10 minutes | Search a room, Breather |
| Hour | 6 Scenes | Burn out a torch, research |
| Shift | 6 hours | Travel, Rest |
| Day | 4 Shifts | Recover from Fatigue |

### Movement

Speed indicates how far you can Move on your Turn. Each battlemat square = 5 feet.

**Speed by Dexterity:**

| DEX | Speed | Crawl | Travel |
|-|-|-|-|
| 2-3 | 25' | 75' | 5mi |
| 4-5 | 30' | 90' | 6mi |
| 6-7 | 35' | 105' | 7mi |

**Speed Types:**

| Type | Description |
|-|-|
| Climb | Full Speed while climbing (others are Vulnerable) |
| Cling | As Climb, plus ceilings |
| Fly | Move through the air at full Speed |
| Phase | Move in occupied space; forced out at Turn end (5 damage) |
| Swim | Full Speed swimming (others Vulnerable in deep liquid) |

**Difficult Terrain:** 2 feet of movement per 1 foot.
**Falling:** 1d6 per 10 feet (max 20d6 at 200 feet), land Prone.

### Focusing

Some effects stay active over time via Focus. Ends if you drop it or Focus on another effect.

### Position & Size

| Size | Height | Occupies |
|-|-|-|
| Small | <3 ft | 1 unit* |
| Medium | 3-10 ft | 1 unit |
| Large | 11-20 ft | 1-4 units |
| Huge | 21-40 ft | 5-9 units |
| Giant | 41-60 ft | 10-16 units |
| Colossal | >60 ft | 17+ units |

*Small doesn't prevent movement through its space.*

Can't Move through a Foe's space unless at least two sizes larger.

**Flanking:** If 2+ Allied Beings are Close to a Foe, the Foe is Vulnerable (if no more than one size larger than the Allies).

**Grappling:**
- **Begin (Hero):** Attack Action + passed Brawl Check.
- **Begin (Enemy):** Melee Attack that Restrains.
- **End (Hero):** Action for Endure or Reflex Save; pass = free + half Speed back.
- **End (Enemy):** Action; Hero makes Brawl Check to maintain.
- Grappler's Speed halved unless Target is smaller.

### Rest

Requires a Shift of light/no activity. If fed and rested: restore HP, Luck, and Mana to max. If HP already max: remove 1 Fatigue.

**Breather:** Once per Shift, eat a Ration + drink water to regain HP equal to Might.

| Lodging | Cost/Day |
|-|-|
| Horrible | 1s |
| Poor | 2s |
| Modest | 10s |
| Comfortable | 20s |
| Luxury | 40s |
| Opulent | 1g |

### Temperature

Each Shift in Bitter Cold or Sweltering Heat requires an Endure Save. Fail = 1 Fatigue. Immune to Cold/Fire damage = auto-pass for the corresponding temperature.

### Damaging Objects

Attacks against unequipped Objects auto-hit.

| Material | Armor |
|-|-|
| Fragile (cloth, paper, crystal, glass) | 0 |
| Light (hide, leather, wood) | 1 |
| Medium (bone, chain, stone, wood) | 2 |
| Heavy (metal) | 3 |
| Dense (+1 metal) | 4 |
| Titanic (+2 metal) | 5 |
| Near-Indestructible (+3 metal) | 6 |

| Size | HP |
|-|-|
| Small | 1 |
| Medium | 4 |
| Large | 16 |
| Huge | 50 |
| Giant | 200 |
| Colossal | 1000 |

### Progress Clocks

Track dynamic plot points. 4-segment Clocks track Shifts in a day; 6-segment Clocks track Scenes in a Crawl.

### Statuses

| Status | Effect |
|-|-|
| Berserk | Can't Focus; only Attack, Move, Rush. No Morale Checks. Can't be Frightened |
| Blinded | Can't see; Vulnerable |
| Burning | Takes indicated damage at start of Turns unless ended (e.g., dousing fire) |
| Charmed | Can't willingly attack the charmer |
| Confused | Checks/Saves Hindered; Saves against its Actions Favored |
| Dazed | Can't Focus or Move unless uses Action to do so |
| Fatigued | Each Fatigue = 1 Item Slot. 3+ = can't Rush. 5 = death |
| Frightened | -2 penalty to damage dealt |
| Incapacitated | Can't Focus, Act, or Move. Fails all MIT/DEX Checks. Vulnerable |
| Invisible | Can't be seen by normal senses; those who can't see it act as Blinded |
| Paralyzed | Incapacitated; Speed is 0 |
| Prone | Stand/drop costs 10' Speed. Crawl: 2' per 1'. Can't Rush. Vulnerable to Melee/Dodge |
| Restrained | Vulnerable; Speed is 0 |
| Sickened | -2 penalty to healing received. May have additional effects in parenthesis |
| Suffocating | Heroes: roll d8 each Round, >= Might = 1 Fatigue. Enemies: 1 Fatigue/Round |
| Unconscious | Blinded, Incapacitated, Prone. Close Attacks always Crit |
| Vulnerable | Attack Checks and Saves against Attacks Hindered. Attacks against it Favored |

### Combat

Combat begins when a Being physically harms another. Time = Rounds (~10 seconds each), broken into Turns per Group.

**Turn Order:** Heroes go first. If risk of surprise, Heroes must pass Detect Check; failures can't act on first Turn.

**Actions (choose one per Turn):**

| Action | Effect |
|-|-|
| Attack | Make Attack Check with Weapon, deal damage on pass |
| Cast | Cast a Spell |
| Hold | Use an Action or Move on an Off-Turn |
| Jump | Spend half Speed to leap that distance horizontally |
| Rush | Gain extra Speed equal to your Speed this Turn |
| Use | Interact with or use an Object |

**Move:** Speed refreshes at start of Turn. Break up movement as you like.

**Defending:**
- **Block:** Endure Save (Hindered if attack is Ranged).
- **Dodge:** Reflex Save (Hindered if wearing Heavy Armor).
- On a passed Save, ignore one of the attack's highest rolled damage dice.

**Ending Combat:**
- Death, Surrender, or Flee.
- **Retreating:** Spend Action and Move to flee. Combat ends after that Turn unless chased.

### Running Enemies

**Zone** determines movement behavior:
- **Frontline:** Moves Close for Melee.
- **Midline:** Moves Close to Attack, then Near after. At half HP or lower, acts as Backline.
- **Backline:** Stays Far for Ranged/Casts.

**Morale:** Roll 2d6 at:
- First death in Group
- HP at half or less, or half Group Incapacitated

If 2d6 > Morale score, Group retreats or surrenders.

**Action Gambit:** Actions listed in priority order; use topmost available.
**Multi Actions:** Take multiple listed Actions with one Action.
**Recharge:** Countdown die rolled each Round to regain ability.

## Exploration

### Crawl (Dungeon Crawl)

Small-scope exploration, measured in Scenes.

- **Crawl Clock:** 6-segment. Fills on Scene change or loud noise.
- **Crawl Speed:** 3x base Speed.
- **Random Encounters:** Triggered when a Clock segment fills.
- **Break Down Door:** Damage per Object rules (p. 12). Makes noise.
- **Lockpicking:** Finesse Check. Fail = lockpick broken + noise.
- **Spot Hidden:** Search during a Scene in a 10' area to find hidden things.

**Traps:**

| Trap | Save | Damage & Effect |
|-|-|-|
| Blade | Reflex | d8 |
| Cave-In | Reflex | 3d6 and Restrained |
| Darts | Reflex | d6 |
| Dread Wave | Will | Frightened (1 Hour unless dispelled) |
| Electric Shock | Endure | d6 and Dazed (1 Round) |
| Flame | Reflex | Burning (Cd6) |
| Gas | Endure | Sickened (Cd6 damage) |
| Psychic Stun | Will | Paralyzed (1 Hour unless dispelled) |
| Snare | Reflex | Restrained |
| Spikes | Reflex | d6 (x2 if falling) |

**Lowest Luck:** If unclear who is Targeted by a Trap, default to Hero(es) with lowest Luck.

### Travel

Large-scope exploration, measured in Shifts.

**Procedure:**
1. **Move:** Appoint Navigator, determine Pace, move into new Hex (each Hex = 6 miles).
2. **Complications:** Roll on table. Pace affects roll.
3. **Survey:** Reveal adjacent unrevealed Hexes.

**Travel Pace:**

| Pace | Multiplier | Complication Roll |
|-|-|-|
| Slow | x0.5 | Add d6, use two highest |
| Normal | x1 | Normal |
| Fast | x2 | Add d6, use two lowest |

Travel Speed = Speed / 5 (miles per Shift at Normal Pace).

**Limitations:** After Rest, can Travel 2 Shifts. Each additional Shift = 1 Fatigue.

**Hazardous Lands:** Jungles, Mountains, Swamps halve travel miles per Shift.

**Complications (d66):**

| d66 | Complication |
|-|-|
| 11 | Hazard: Reflex Saves or 2d6 damage |
| 12 | Lost: Navigator Survival Check or return to previous hex |
| 13 | Obscurement: Hinders sight Checks beyond Near for d6 Shifts |
| 14-15 | Storm: Difficult Terrain for Cd4 Shifts |
| 16 | Temperature: Sweltering/Cold for Cd4 Shifts |
| 21-66 | No complication |

## Downtime

Activities generally take a Shift.

### Craft

Requires Materials worth at least half Item value + reasonable tools.

| Craft Difficulty | Value/Shift |
|-|-|
| 18-17 | 1s |
| 16-15 | 20s |
| 14-13 | 50s |
| 12-11 | 2g 50s |
| 10-9 | 5g |
| 8-7 | 7g 50s |
| 6 | 10g |

**Scrapping:** Item can be scrapped for materials worth half its value.

### Foraging

Spend a Shift (or Travel at Slow Pace). Survival Check:
- **Food:** d6 Rations
- **Materials:** (d6 x 5s) worth

### Hunting

Spend a Shift. Survival Check to find Wild Game. On a Crit, choose which Beast.

| d6 | Beast | Rations |
|-|-|-|
| 1 | 2d6 Wolf | 9 |
| 2 | d6 Fowl (as Hawk) | 1 |
| 3 | 2d6 Vermin (as Rabbit) | 1 |
| 4 | d6 Deer | 4 |
| 5 | d6 Boar | 13 |
| 6 | d4 Cattle | 18 |

Harvesting: Rations or Materials equal to HP. Materials value = HP x 5s.

### Study

Gain a Studied die (spend for Favor on a d20 roll next day, before resolution). Can also change a Class Feature choice (e.g., Spell Known).

## Ancestries

### Human
- **Type:** Humanlike | **Size:** Medium
- **Knack:** Gain a Perk and a Training.
- **Strong Potential:** Increase one Stat by 1 (max 7).

### Dwarf
- **Type:** Humanlike | **Size:** Medium
- **Darksight:** Not Blinded by Dark.
- **Sturdy:** +1 bonus on Saves against Frightened, Sickened, or Shoved.
- **Tough:** Bonus to max HP equal to Level.

### Elf
- **Type:** Fae | **Size:** Medium
- **Ascendancy:** Trained in Arcana, Mysticism, Influence, or Ranged.
- **Elven Eyes:** Favor on sight-based Detect Checks.
- **Naturally Attuned:** Know a Spell, Cast with a Skill of your choice.

### Halfling
- **Type:** Humanlike | **Size:** Medium
- **Nimble:** +5 ft Speed; ignore Hinder on Reflex Saves if not Incapacitated.
- **Squat:** Move through spaces occupied by other Beings.
- **Tricksy:** Gain 1 additional Luck from Rest.

### Draken
- **Type:** Cryptid | **Size:** Medium
- **Breath Attack:** 15-foot Cone, Endure or Will Save, 2d6! damage. Recharges on Rest or 1 Fatigue.
- **Scale:** +1 Armor Rating.
- **Draconic Resilience:** Half damage from Acid, Cold, Fire, or Shock (choose one).

### Goblin
- **Type:** Cryptid | **Size:** Medium
- **Darksight:** Not Blinded by Dark.
- **Nimble:** +5 ft Speed; ignore Hinder on Reflex Saves if not Incapacitated.
- **Scavenger:** Favor on Endure Saves against Sickened.

### Orc
- **Type:** Cryptid | **Size:** Medium
- **Darksight:** Not Blinded by Dark.
- **Beefy:** Favor on Saves against Grappled/Shoved; Favor on Grapple/Shove Checks.
- **Hulking:** +2 Item Slots.

## Classes

### Leveling Up

Each Level:
- Max HP increases (Might x Level)
- New/improved Class Features
- Even Levels: increase a Stat by 1 (max 7)
- Odd Levels after 1st: gain a Perk

**Leveling Methods:**

| Method | Description |
|-|-|
| XP Questionnaire | After each session, 1 XP per "yes": completed Quest? Failed and let it resolve? Passed Hindered Check? Made a discovery? Looted 50g+? |
| Destiny | GM creates 9 Milestones; each = 1 Level for all Heroes |

**Level Pacing:**

| Pace | XP/Level | Est. Campaign Length |
|-|-|-|
| Quick | 5 per Level | 2-5 months |
| Normal | 5 x next Level | 5-12 months |
| Epic | 7 x next Level | 1-2 years |
| Saga | 10 x next Level | 2+ years |

**Starting at Level 0:** Choose Ancestry, Stats, Gear. Max HP as Level 1. No Class until Level 1.

### Alchemist

*Low defense, high damage/utility blaster. Stay Far, use alchemical items.*

- **Action:** Use (Craft & Attack) | **Move:** Backline | **Key Stat:** Reason
- **Training:** Weapons: -- | Skills: Craft and 4 others

| Level | Features |
|-|-|
| 1st | Alchemy, Catalyze |
| 2nd | Eureka |
| 3rd | Perk |
| 4th | Potency |
| 5th | Perk |
| 6th | Mix |
| 7th | Perk |
| 8th | Big Bang |
| 9th | Perk |
| 10th | Prima Materia |

**Alchemy (1st):** Attack with alchemical items using Craft. Choose 4 Formulae (value up to Alchemist Level x 50s). Only need 5s materials to Craft these + Alchemy Tools. Learn 1 more every 2 Levels.

**Catalyze (1st):** Gain Deft Hands Perk. Can Craft alchemical items with Use Action.

**Eureka (2nd):** Gain a Studied die when you Crit on a Craft Check.

**Potency (4th):** Damage/healing dice of alchemical items can explode.

**Mix (6th):** Use Action to combine two alchemical items; both effects occur. Combined item lasts 1 Round.

**Big Bang (8th):** +d6 bonus to damage/healing of alchemical items; explode on two highest values.

**Prima Materia (10th):** Once per Day, Action or skip Move to Craft an alchemical item (up to 10g value) without materials.

### Barbarian

*Medium-high defense bruiser. Frontline, hit hard.*

- **Action:** Attack (Melee) | **Move:** Frontline | **Key Stat:** Might
- **Training:** Weapons: Melee | Skills: Brawl and 3 from Detect, Influence, Leadership, Mysticism, or Survival

| Level | Features |
|-|-|
| 1st | Rage, Wrath |
| 2nd | Aggressor |
| 3rd | Perk |
| 4th | Fearmonger |
| 5th | Perk |
| 6th | Mindless Rancor |
| 7th | Perk |
| 8th | Bloodthirsty |
| 9th | Perk |
| 10th | Rip and Tear |

**Rage (1st):** While Berserk in Light/no Armor: reduce damage taken by 1 per die, attack damage dice one size larger and can explode. Go Berserk after taking damage or as part of attacking. Lasts 1 minute.

**Wrath (1st):** Gain Interceptor Perk. Can trigger against Ranged Attack, Cast, or damage to you/Ally.

**Aggressor (2nd):** +10 ft Speed on first Round of Combat. 3+ Fatigue doesn't prevent Rush.

**Fearmonger (4th):** On kill, every Near Enemy with HD < your Level is Frightened until end of next Turn.

**Mindless Rancor (6th):** Can't be Charmed, Confused, or compelled against will.

**Bloodthirsty (8th):** Attacks Favored against Beings missing any HP. Sense them within Far (Blindsight).

**Rip and Tear (10th):** While Berserk, reduce damage by 2 per die (not 1), +1 bonus per damage die dealt.

### Bard

*Low/mid defense, high support. Optimize Group d20 rolls.*

- **Action:** Virtuoso | **Move:** Midline/Backline | **Key Stat:** Presence
- **Training:** Weapons: -- | Skills: Performance and 4 others

| Level | Features |
|-|-|
| 1st | Virtuoso, Well-Versed |
| 2nd | Song of Rest |
| 3rd | Perk |
| 4th | Starstruck |
| 5th | Perk |
| 6th | Bravado |
| 7th | Perk |
| 8th | Awe-Inspiring |
| 9th | Perk |
| 10th | Encore |

**Virtuoso (1st):** Action or skip Move to perform with Musical Instrument. Allies that hear gain Favor on chosen rolls until next Turn: Inspiration (healing, d6 bonus), Resolve (Saves), or Valor (Attack/Cast Checks).

**Well-Versed (1st):** Ignore Prerequisites for Perks. Take a Perk now.

**Song of Rest (2nd):** During Breather, you and Allies gain Studied die + regain extra HP = Presence + Bard Level.

**Starstruck (4th):** During Virtuoso, choose Near Enemy; Performance Check. Pass = Berserk, Charmed, Confused, or Frightened (Cd4 Rounds).

**Bravado (6th):** Will Saves can't be Hindered (if not Incapacitated). Ignore hearing-based effects.

**Awe-Inspiring (8th):** Virtuoso grants two Favor.

**Encore (10th):** Starstruck can affect all Near Enemies.

| 1st | Fleet of Foot, Step Up |

## Spells

### Casting Spells

To Cast: hands must be open, or holding spellbook, staff, or trinket.

**When Casting, determine:**
1. **Damage, Effect, or both?** Only d6 damage or only Effect = no Mana. Both = 1 Mana.
2. **Delivery** (who is Targeted).
3. **Duration** (how long it lasts).

**Cast Checks:** Only required if an unwilling Being is a Target.

**Damage:** Base d6. Spend Mana for +1d6 per Mana.

**Effect:** What the Spell subjects the Target to (unless damage-only).

**Duration:** Instant damage; effects last until next Turn unless you Focus. Focused = lasts until Focus ends. Unwilling Targets require 1 Mana + Cast Check each Round to maintain Focus.

**Continual:** Doesn't require Focus; lasts until dismissed (no Action).

### Delivery

| Delivery | Base Cost | Cost Increase |
|-|-|-|
| Aura | 2 | +1 per +5' radius |
| Cone | 2 | +2 per additional 5' length |
| Cube | 1 | +1 per additional 5' cube |
| Imbue | 0 | +2 per additional Target |
| Glyph | 2 | Special |
| Line | 2 | +1 per 10' extra length |
| Remote | 0 | +1 per additional Target |
| Sphere | 2 | +1 per +5' radius |
| Touch | 0 | -- |

- **Aura:** 10' radius from you, affecting Targets of choice.
- **Cone:** 15' cone in front of you.
- **Cube:** 5' cube you can see.
- **Glyph:** 5' glyph on Target; Spell cast from glyph when you choose.
- **Imbue:** Target a Weapon within Far. On hit, spend 1 Mana to Cast as Touch (no Action).
- **Line:** 5' wide, 30' long, 10' tall. Doubling width/height doubles cost.
- **Remote:** Ambience or bolt on a Target.
- **Sphere:** 5' radius sphere in sight.
- **Touch:** Close Target or self.

## Bestiary

### Reading Statblocks

Presented top to bottom:
- **Threat Level (TL):** Rough Hero Level equivalent.
- **Hit Dice (HD):** HP = HD x 4.5. Small Beings = 1 HP per HD.
- **Speed:** Movement + Speed Types.
- **# Appearing:** Average count in random encounters.
- **Senses:** Special senses beyond normal.
- **Armor:** Damage reduction (descriptor in brackets for system conversion).
- **Immune/Weak:** Damage immunities and weaknesses.
- **Zone:** Frontline / Midline / Backline.
- **Morale:** 2d6; if result >= Morale, retreats. No Morale = fights to death.
- **Actions:** Priority-ordered. Multi-Attack, Melee/Ranged Attacks, Casts.
- **Abilities:** Combat-relevant abilities.

**Boss HP:** Double HP for boss monsters.

**Enemies don't roll.** Their Checks/Saves are assumed to pass unless Heroes succeed.

**Enemies don't track Mana.** Limited uses or Recharge instead.

### Threat Level

TL is a loose measure. Not perfectly balanced -- meant as a guideline.

**Building Encounters:**
- Compare cumulative TL of enemies vs Heroes' Levels.
- Adjust HP (double for bosses, halve for minions).
- Use terrain, positioning, and numbers to vary difficulty.

### Being Types

- **Humanlike** (Humans, Dwarves, Elves, Halflings, etc.)
- **Artificials** (Constructs, Golems, Homunculi, etc.)
- **Beasts** (Natural animals and mundane creatures)
- **Cryptids** (Draken, Goblins, Orcs, Giants, Trolls, Dragons, etc.)
- **Fae** (Elves, Pixies, Dryads, Satyrs, etc.)
- **Outers** (Aberrations, Demons, Devils, extraplanar entities)
- **Primordials** (Elementals, Oozes, Treants, etc.)
- **Undead** (Ghosts, Skeletons, Vampires, Zombies, Liches, etc.)

3. **Travel:** Journey to the Quest location.
4. **Crawl:** Dungeon exploration at the Quest site.
5. **Confrontation:** The climactic encounter.
6. **Aftermath:** Consequences, rewards, follow-up hooks.
